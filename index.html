My Project - Investment Yield Calculator

In recent years, my husband and I have undergone a shift in our mindset and decided to enter the world of investments.
We spent an entire year learning about the field – its terminology, different tracks, and various approaches to financial management.
We made the decision to find the path that best suits us, aiming for a complete transformation of our financial lives.
The sentence that truly shook us was from someone online who said:
“It doesn’t make sense to live from paycheck to paycheck...”
That was the moment we realized there must be a different way, something better we had to strive for.
This is where the idea for the project was born – and with it, a significant turning point in our financial journey.
