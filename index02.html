About the Project

This project is an investment yield calculator that helps users estimate potential returns across three different investment tracks over a specified period of time.

  • Conservative Track: Designed for long-term investors who prefer minimal risk. Lower potential returns, higher stability.
  • Balanced Track: A medium-risk, medium-return option for those wanting a balance between safety and gain.
  • Aggressive Track: Short-term, high-risk, high-return option for adventurous investors.

The goal is to help users understand how risk levels and investment durations influence returns.
